# Returns, Shipping & Fulfillment (physical goods add-on)

Some customers purchase our branded hardware (e.g. the Acme Tag scanner).
This page covers the physical-goods policy for those orders.

## Shipping

- **EU**: free standard shipping on orders over €100. Delivery 2–4 business days.
- **US**: free standard shipping on orders over $100. Delivery 3–6 business days.
- **Rest of the world**: calculated at checkout. Delivery 7–14 business days.

Orders placed before 14:00 CET ship the same business day.

## Returns

You can return any unused, undamaged item within **30 days** of delivery
for a full refund. Opened items are accepted only if they are defective.

Steps:

1. Open a return ticket at https://acmecloud.example/returns with your
   order number.
2. Print the prepaid return label we email you.
3. Drop off the package at the nearest carrier point within 14 days.
4. Refund is issued within 5 business days after we receive the item.

## Warranty

Hardware is covered by a **2-year limited warranty** against manufacturing
defects. Damage from drops, water, or unauthorized repairs is not covered.

## Lost or damaged packages

If tracking shows "delivered" but you did not receive the package, wait
24 hours (carriers sometimes mark it early), then contact us. For visibly
damaged packages, take photos and open a ticket within 48 hours.
