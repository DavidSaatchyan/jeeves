# About Acme Cloud

Acme Cloud is a SaaS platform for small and mid-sized businesses that want
to automate their sales, support, and billing workflows in one place.

- Founded: 2019
- Headquarters: Berlin, Germany
- Employees: 180+ across 14 countries
- Customers: 12,000+ active accounts
- Languages supported in the product UI: English, German, French, Spanish,
  Portuguese, Russian, Turkish.

## Our mission

Help businesses spend less time on operations and more time on their customers.
