# Support Policy & SLA

## Response time by plan

| Plan       | First-response SLA | Channels                    |
|------------|--------------------|-----------------------------|
| Starter    | 24 business hours  | Email, in-app chat          |
| Business   | 4 business hours   | Email, chat, phone (EU/US)  |
| Enterprise | 1 hour, 24x7       | All channels + dedicated CSM|

Business hours: Monday to Friday, 09:00–18:00 in the customer's timezone,
excluding public holidays in the office that handles the ticket.

## Severity levels

- **S1 — Critical**: production is down for all users. Target restore: 4h (Enterprise).
- **S2 — High**: major feature unusable, no workaround. Target: 1 business day.
- **S3 — Normal**: bug with workaround, or how-to question. Target: 3 business days.
- **S4 — Low**: feature request, cosmetic issue. Target: best effort.

## Escalation

If a ticket is not progressing, customers can reply with the word
**ESCALATE** in the subject line, which forwards it to the support
team lead. Enterprise customers have their CSM's direct email.

## Maintenance windows

Scheduled maintenance happens on the last Saturday of each month,
02:00–04:00 UTC. Customers are notified 7 days in advance via the
status page and email.
