# Onboarding in 15 minutes

Follow these steps right after signing up:

1. **Create your workspace.** Give it a name and choose a timezone.
   This determines invoice dates and scheduled reports.

2. **Invite your team.** *Settings → Members → Invite*. You can paste
   a list of emails separated by commas. Each invitee receives an email
   with a sign-up link that expires in 7 days.

3. **Connect your data sources.** Most customers start with:
   - Gmail or Outlook (for email threads)
   - Slack (for internal context)
   - Stripe or your billing provider (for revenue data)

4. **Import contacts.** Either upload a CSV (*Contacts → Import*) or
   connect your CRM. Mapping is automatic for standard columns
   (name, email, phone, company) and adjustable for custom fields.

5. **Create your first automation.** Templates available:
   - "Welcome new customer" — sends a welcome email on sign-up.
   - "Churn warning" — pings CSM if a customer's usage drops >30%.
   - "Invoice reminder" — sends a reminder 3 days before invoice due.

6. **Install the browser extension** (optional) for one-click saving of
   conversations from Gmail and LinkedIn into Acme Cloud.

When you are done, the onboarding checklist on the dashboard will show
5/5 completed. This unlocks the *Advanced Automations* feature.
