# Pricing & Plans

All plans are billed monthly or annually (annual = 2 months free).
Prices below are in USD; VAT is added where applicable.

## Starter — $19 / user / month

- Up to 5 users
- 5 GB document storage
- Email support (24h response)
- Basic integrations: Gmail, Slack, Zapier
- 10,000 API calls / month

## Business — $49 / user / month

- Up to 50 users
- 100 GB document storage
- Priority email + chat support (4h response)
- All Starter integrations + HubSpot, Salesforce, Stripe
- 100,000 API calls / month
- Custom roles and permissions
- SSO via Google Workspace / Microsoft Entra ID

## Enterprise — custom pricing (from $12,000 / year)

- Unlimited users
- Unlimited storage
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager
- 99.9% uptime SLA with financial credits
- SAML SSO, SCIM user provisioning
- Advanced audit log and DLP
- On-premise or private cloud deployment option

## Free trial

Every new account gets a **14-day free trial of the Business plan**, no
credit card required. After the trial, the account is automatically moved
to Starter unless an Enterprise contract is signed.

## Discounts

- Non-profits: 30% off all plans (requires proof of status)
- Education: 50% off Starter and Business
- Annual pre-pay: 2 months free (equivalent to ~17% discount)

## Refund policy

Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime; no refunds for the current month.
Annual plans are refunded pro-rata within the first 30 days.
