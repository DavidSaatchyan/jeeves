Capabilities
Not just a chatbot.
A full support stack.
Everything you need to automate customer support without losing the human touch.

RAG-powered knowledge

Upload PDFs, docs, FAQs — Jeeves retrieves with cosine similarity. Every answer has a source. No hallucinations.

CRM context

Connect HubSpot or any REST API. Jeeves sees customer name, tariff, order history, and past tickets instantly.

Custom actions
