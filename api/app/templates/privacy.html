Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

1. What We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our Service:

  • Account data: email address, company name, password hash
  • Usage data: conversation logs, channel configurations, integration settings
  • Knowledge base content: documents and files you upload for AI processing
  • Technical data: IP addresses, browser type, device info (for security and analytics)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing and operating the AI support agent service
  • Processing conversations between your customers and the AI agent
  • Improving response accuracy through retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
  • Billing and account management
  • Security monitoring and fraud prevention

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We share data only with:

  • AI model providers: conversations are sent to LLM APIs (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) for processing. These providers have their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.
  • Infrastructure providers: hosting, database, and storage services under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Your Knowledge Base Data

Documents and files you upload are processed to generate AI responses. We:

  • Do not use your uploaded content to train our base AI models
  • Encrypt data at rest and in transit
  • Delete your data within 30 days of account termination
  • Allow you to export or delete your data at any time

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes. Analytics are anonymized where possible.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time. Active conversation logs are retained for up to 12 months for audit and quality purposes unless you configure a shorter retention period.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@jeeves.ai.

9. Children

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated at least 30 days before they take effect.

11. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@jeeves.ai.
