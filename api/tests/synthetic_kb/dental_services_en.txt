Dentistry Services

Our dental department provides comprehensive oral healthcare for adults and children aged 3 and above. We offer preventive, restorative, cosmetic, and surgical dentistry in our modern clinic equipped with digital X-ray and intraoral scanners.

Dental Checkup: €80 — Includes a thorough oral examination, periodontal probing, oral cancer screening, and a dental hygiene assessment. Digital X-rays (€30 per set) are taken as needed. Children's checkups are €50. We recommend checkups every 6 months.

Professional Teeth Cleaning (Scaling and Polish): €100 — Removal of plaque, tartar, and surface stains using ultrasonic scaler and air-polishing with glycine powder. Includes fluoride varnish application. Duration: 30–45 minutes. We recommend cleaning every 6–12 months depending on oral health status.

Dental Fillings: €120–€200 per tooth — Composite (tooth-coloured) fillings for cavities. Price varies by size and location of the filling. Posterior molars with extensive decay requiring multi-surface fillings are at the higher end. Amalgam fillings are available on request at €80 but are no longer our standard recommendation.

Dental Crown: €800 — Porcelain-fused-to-metal or zirconia crowns, including impressions, temporary crown, and laboratory fees. Two appointments required: preparation and impression (60 minutes), and fitting (30 minutes). Metal crowns are €600. Same-day CEREC crowns are available at €950.

Dental Implant: €2,000 — Single tooth implant including the titanium implant fixture, abutment, and porcelain crown. The process requires 3–4 appointments over 3–6 months: initial consultation with CBCT scan (€150), implant placement surgery (€1,200), osseointegration healing period (3–6 months), and crown fitting (€650). Bone grafting if required is €400–€800 extra. All-on-4 or All-on-6 implant treatments are available from €8,000 per arch.
