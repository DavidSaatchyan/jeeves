Gynecology Services

Our gynecology department offers comprehensive women's health services in a comfortable and confidential environment. All examinations are performed by our experienced female and male gynaecologists, and chaperones are available upon request.

Annual Well-Woman Exam: €120 — Includes a full medical history review, breast examination, pelvic examination, Pap smear collection, and discussion of any concerns. Bimanual examination assesses the uterus and ovaries. We recommend annual visits for all women from age 18 or from the onset of sexual activity.

Contraceptive Counselling and Services: Our clinicians provide evidence-based counselling on all contraceptive methods including combined oral contraceptives, progestin-only pills, contraceptive implants (Implanon NXT insertion €200), hormonal IUDs (Mirena/Kyleena insertion €350), copper IUDs (insertion €250), contraceptive injection, vaginal ring, and permanent sterilisation. A counselling session costs €80 and includes a thorough medical and family history assessment to determine the most suitable method.

IUD Insertion and Removal: IUD insertion costs €250 for copper IUDs and €350 for hormonal IUDs (Mirena, Kyleena). The price includes pre-procedure counselling, the device, insertion, and a follow-up ultrasound to confirm correct placement. Insertion is typically performed during menstruation for easier cervical access. Patients may experience cramping during and after insertion — mild analgesia is recommended. Removal costs €80.

Prenatal Care: We offer full antenatal care including confirmation of pregnancy (blood hCG and ultrasound), booking visits, routine screening (first-trimester combined screening, anomaly scan at 20 weeks, glucose tolerance test at 24–28 weeks), and postnatal checks. A standard prenatal visit costs €100. The complete antenatal package (10 visits as per national guidelines) is €900.

Additional services: menopause management and HRT counselling (€120), fertility assessments (€180 including AMH and ultrasound), and treatment for recurrent urinary tract infections (€80).
