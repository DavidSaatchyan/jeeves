Botox Aftercare Instructions

Following your Botox treatment at our clinic, please adhere to these aftercare guidelines to ensure optimal results and minimise the risk of complications.

For the first 24 hours after treatment: avoid any strenuous exercise or physical activity that raises your heart rate. Do not lie down for at least 4 hours following the injections — remain in an upright position to prevent the toxin from migrating to unintended areas. Refrain from touching, rubbing, or massaging the treated areas for 24 hours. Avoid facial treatments, saunas, steam rooms, and excessive heat exposure for 48 hours.

Results typically become visible within 3–7 days and last between 3 to 4 months. We recommend scheduling your next appointment at the 3-month mark to maintain consistent results. Common temporary side effects include mild redness, swelling, or bruising at the injection sites. If you experience any unusual symptoms such as drooping eyelids or difficulty swallowing, contact our clinic immediately.
