Laser Hair Removal Protocol

Our clinic uses the Candela GentleMax Pro laser system, combining Alexandrite and Nd:YAG wavelengths to treat all skin types safely and effectively. A patch test is required 24–48 hours before the first full treatment to assess skin response and determine optimal energy settings.

A course of 6 to 8 sessions scheduled 4–6 weeks apart is recommended for optimal hair reduction. Maintenance sessions once or twice per year may be needed thereafter. Results vary by skin type, hair colour, and hormonal factors.

Contraindications: pregnancy and breastfeeding, active infections in the treatment area, photosensitivity disorders, isotretinoin use within the last 6 months, and history of keloid scarring. Sun exposure and self-tanning products must be avoided for 4 weeks before and after treatment. Shave the treatment area 24 hours before each session — do not wax, pluck, or use depilatory creams between sessions as the laser targets the hair follicle pigment.
