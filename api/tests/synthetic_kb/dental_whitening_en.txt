Professional Teeth Whitening

Our clinic offers two professional teeth whitening options to suit different needs and budgets. All treatments are performed or supervised by our qualified dental team and use only medically approved whitening agents.

In-Office Whitening (€350): A single 90-minute appointment using a 40% hydrogen peroxide gel activated by a specialised LED lamp. The gums are protected with a barrier, and the gel is applied in three 15-minute cycles. Results are immediately visible, with teeth typically lightening by 4–8 shades. A desensitising gel is applied at the end of the session to minimise post-treatment sensitivity. Patients may experience temporary tooth sensitivity for 24–48 hours following treatment.

Take-Home Whitening Kit (€180): Custom-fitted trays are created from dental impressions taken during a 30-minute appointment. Patients receive 10% carbamide peroxide gel syringes and are instructed to wear the trays for 2–4 hours daily or overnight for 10–14 days. Results are gradual and typically last 12–18 months with proper maintenance. Refill gel syringes are available at €40 per set.

Both methods deliver results lasting 12 to 18 months depending on dietary habits (coffee, tea, red wine, and tobacco consumption will accelerate staining). We recommend a professional cleaning before whitening for optimal results. Whitening is not recommended during pregnancy, for patients under 16, or for those with untreated gum disease or severe enamel erosion.
