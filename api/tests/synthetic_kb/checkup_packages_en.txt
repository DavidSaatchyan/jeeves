Preventive Health Check-Up Packages

Our clinic offers three tiers of comprehensive preventive health assessments tailored to different age groups, risk profiles, and health goals. All packages include a detailed consultation with a healthcare professional who reviews your results and provides personalised lifestyle recommendations.

Basic Package — €200
Ideal for adults aged 18–40 with no significant health concerns. Includes: full blood count, comprehensive metabolic panel, lipid profile, fasting glucose, thyroid function (TSH), blood pressure measurement, BMI calculation, and urinalysis. Duration: 45 minutes. Results reviewed during a 15-minute follow-up consultation.

Executive Package — €500
Designed for adults aged 40–60 or those with risk factors such as family history of cardiovascular disease. Includes everything in the Basic package plus: resting ECG, exercise stress test (treadmill), abdominal ultrasound, vitamin D and B12 levels, HbA1c, and a cardiovascular risk assessment (SCORE2). Duration: 2 hours. Includes a 30-minute physician consultation to discuss results.

Comprehensive Package — €800
The most thorough assessment, recommended for adults over 50 or those with multiple health concerns. Includes everything in the Executive package plus: full-body MRI (low-dose, no contrast), colonoscopy with sedation, lung function test, bone density scan (DEXA), tumour marker screening (CEA, CA-19-9, AFP, PSA for men), and a dermatological mole check. Duration: full day (approximately 6 hours). Results are compiled into a detailed report and reviewed in a 45-minute consultation with a senior physician.

All packages require a 48-hour fasting period before blood work. Reports are delivered within 5–7 working days.
