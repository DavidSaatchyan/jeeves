Common Dermatology Treatments

Our dermatology department offers a full range of medical and aesthetic dermatology services. All treatments are performed by board-certified dermatologists. Below are our most commonly requested services.

Acne Treatment: We offer a personalised approach combining topical therapies (retinoids, benzoyl peroxide, topical antibiotics) with in-clinic procedures. Laser acne treatment (Nd:YAG or IPL) costs €150–€300 per session, with 3–6 sessions typically required. Chemical peels for acne-prone skin start at €120 per session. Severe or cystic acne may be treated with oral isotretinoin under close medical supervision. Price for a consultation and prescription: €100.

Mole Check (Total Body Dermoscopy): A full-body skin examination using a dermatoscope. Includes photographic documentation and digital mole mapping. Recommended annually for all patients, and every 6 months for those with atypical mole syndrome or a family history of melanoma. Price: €80. Suspicious lesions can be removed on the same day — shave excision €120, excision with sutures from €200 (includes histology).

Eczema Management: Treatment plans include emollients, topical corticosteroids, calcineurin inhibitors, and phototherapy (narrowband UVB). Severe refractory cases may be treated with systemic immunosuppressants or biologic therapy (dupilumab). Follow-up consultations: €60. Phototherapy sessions: €50 per session, typically 2–3 sessions per week for 6–12 weeks.

Psoriasis Treatment: Options include topical therapies, phototherapy, systemic treatments (methotrexate, cyclosporine), and biologic agents (adalimumab, secukinumab, ixekizumab). Biologic therapy requires prior authorisation and baseline screening. A psoriasis severity assessment including PASI and DLQI scoring is performed at every visit. Consultation: €80.
