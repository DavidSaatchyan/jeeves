General Practice Services

Our general practice department provides comprehensive primary care services for patients of all ages. We are open Monday to Friday 08:00–18:00 and Saturday 09:00–13:00. Same-day appointments are available for urgent concerns.

Standard GP Consultation: €60 — A 15-minute appointment for acute illness, chronic disease management, or medication review. Extended consultations (30 minutes) for complex cases are €100. Our lead GP, Dr. Müller, consults in both English and German.

Blood Tests (Phlebotomy): €30 — Includes sample collection and standard laboratory processing. Results are typically available within 24–48 hours. Fasting (8–12 hours) is required for glucose and lipid panels. Additional tests such as vitamin levels, hormone panels, or allergy screening are charged at cost plus €10 handling fee per test.

Vaccinations: €40 per dose — We offer routine vaccinations including influenza (seasonal, €40), tetanus-diphtheria-polio booster (€40), hepatitis A and B, HPV, pneumococcal, and travel-related vaccines. Please book a 10-minute nurse appointment for vaccine administration. Vaccine prices include the consultation.

Travel Health Advice: €50 — A 20-minute consultation with a travel medicine specialist. Includes review of your itinerary, recommended vaccinations, malaria prophylaxis, and general health precautions for your destination. A written travel health summary is provided. Please book at least 4–6 weeks before departure to allow time for vaccination schedules.

Additional services: ECGs (€40), spirometry (€50), ear irrigation (€40), wound care and suture removal (€30), and fit notes / medical certificates (€25).
